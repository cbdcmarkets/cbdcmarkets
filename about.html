About CBDC Markets

Tracking the future of central bank digital currencies with transparency and independence.

Our Mission

CBDC Markets is an independent platform designed to provide a clear, transparent, and analytical view of central bank digital currency (CBDC) initiatives across the globe. Our goal is to offer policymakers, researchers, and financial professionals a reliable benchmark for assessing CBDC readiness, adoption, and development progress.

Who We Are

Our team consists of researchers, financial analysts, and technology experts with a focus on digital currencies, payment systems, and central banking. We are committed to delivering unbiased, data-driven insights on the evolution of CBDCs, without representing any government, central bank, or private institution.

Our Approach

  • Independent and transparent methodology for all indices
  • Data sourced from publicly available central bank and regulatory reports
  • Regular updates to reflect current progress and pilot programs
  • Neutral presentation: no investment advice or trading recommendations

Why It Matters

Central bank digital currencies are becoming a foundational layer for the future of payments, settlements, and cross-border transactions. CBDC Markets aims to demystify this evolving landscape and provide a consistent reference point for decision-makers, researchers, and the wider public interested in digital currency innovation.
