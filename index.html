Index Interpretation

The expanded GCRI reflects the relative progress of CBDC initiatives across leading economies. China leads with a live deployment, while India, Brazil, Singapore, and Sweden continue advanced pilot programs. Russia and other research-stage countries are included for completeness.

This index provides a neutral benchmark and does not predict future outcomes or represent tradable instruments.
