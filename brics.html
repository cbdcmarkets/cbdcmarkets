Index Interpretation

The BRICS CBDC Index reflects the relative progress of CBDC initiatives among the five member countries. China leads with a live deployment, while India and Brazil continue advanced pilot programs. Russia and South Africa are at earlier stages of development.

This index provides a neutral benchmark and does not predict outcomes or represent tradable instruments.
